Animation is a bit more special in multimedia. It consists of still images rapidly changing to produce to an illusion of movement. You could say that animation is a mix of other media, it could be a type of image, a mix of text, it can contain audio, or it may be a video. It can be hard to utilize but when done properly it can transmit a greater amount of information in a short amount of time. Take note of how many animations you plan to add to your multimedia designs, because animations are bascially videos, they require alot of space which may limit or interfere with any viewers of your media.

The animation draws the attention of the viewer first, and they instantly get the message that you want them to like and subscribe. They get the message immediately instead of having to read the text label, animations are great for getting the message to the user fast.

When adding animations, we must be careful because they can be very attention grabbing. Animations tend to draw the viewers focus towards it because it is flashy which is good if you want them to notice it, but just make sure that the user will notice the other media elements you've added. It would be fine if the animation is the main element you want them to watch, however if it's placed with text or other elements, it will overshadow them.
